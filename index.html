Psychology-Driven Cold Call Framework

Introduction

This framework is designed to enhance the effectiveness of cold calls by applying psychological principles.

Key Principles

  • Understanding the prospect's mindset.
  • Building rapport quickly.
  • Framing the conversation strategically.

Techniques

Implement the following techniques for successful cold calling:

  1. Active listening and questioning.
  2. Using storytelling.
  3. Creating urgency and value.
